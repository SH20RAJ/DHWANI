## Dhwani Music Club - BIT Mesra

**Overview**

Dhwani is the **music club of Birla Institute of Technology (BIT) Mesra in Ranchi, India**, with a celebrated history of over 60 years of musical excellence. The club serves as the primary organization for promoting and developing musical talent among BIT Mesra students.[1]

- https://dhwani.strivio.world/

**History & Legacy**

The club celebrated its **60th anniversary in 2024**, marking six decades of melodic contributions to the campus and the broader music community. This milestone underscores Dhwani's establishment as one of the most enduring cultural organizations at the institute.[1]

**Activities & Initiatives**

Dhwani orchestrates a diverse range of musical activities and competitions:

**Genres & Instruments Promoted**: The club encourages participation across multiple musical styles including classical singing, Bollywood music, Western music, folk traditions, group singing, fusion bands, orchestras, rock and metal bands, rap, and instrumental performances (tabla, harmonium, guitar, drums, dholak, flute, etc.)[2]

**NAAD Competition**: One of Dhwani's flagship initiatives is **NAAD**, an annual inter-college music competition that has become one of the largest music competitions of its kind in India[3]

**Workshops & Training**: As of October 2025, the club offers comprehensive workshops open to all BIT Mesra students, catering to both beginners and experienced musicians[4][5]

**International Participation**: Members regularly participate in prestigious inter-university cultural festivals and competitions. Notable achievements include:
- 2nd position in RaagSamar (Acoustic unplugged singing) at Kashiyatra 2020, IIT BHU[6]
- 1st position in Sanlayan (Solo instrumental competition) at Kashiyatra 2020, IIT BHU[6]
- 2nd position in Rapmania at IIT Kharagpur's 62nd cultural festival (2021)[6]

**Student Talent Showcase**: The club produces high-quality music videos celebrating global musical diversity, such as the "Colours of Music" World Music Day 2022 production featuring fusion performances across various cultures[7]

**Cultural Impact**

Beyond campus activities, Dhwani has established itself as a gateway for BIT Mesra's musical talent to gain recognition at national and international levels. The club's commitment extends to promoting Indian classical music traditions through partnerships with organizations like **SPIC MACAY** (Society of Indian Classical Music And Art Forms), which promotes classical arts across India and internationally[8]

**Current Status**

As of December 2025, Dhwani continues to remain active with ongoing workshops, social media engagement, and participation in inter-university competitions, maintaining its reputation as a vibrant cultural hub within the BIT Mesra community.
